Welcome to Instox - Your Inventory Management System

Instox is a comprehensive inventory management system designed to help businesses efficiently manage their stock, verify deliveries, and analyze profits. With advanced features like demand forecasting and reduction sales, Instox is tailored to meet the needs of small businesses looking to optimize their inventory operations.

View Inventory

Track current stock levels, manage inventory items, and ensure timely restocking.

Verify Deliveries

Verify the status of deliveries quickly by entering delivery IDs and ensure smooth order processing.

Profit Analysis

Gain insights into your sales and profit data to make informed decisions for business growth.

Demand Forecasting

Leverage advanced machine learning algorithms to predict demand and optimize stock management.

Reduction Sales

Execute reduction sales without sacrificing profit margins, ensuring efficient product turnover.
